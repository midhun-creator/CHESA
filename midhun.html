About CHESA

The Computer Hardware Engineering Students Association (CHESA) is a dynamic student body at Government Polytechnic College, Palakkad, dedicated to fostering technical excellence and innovation in the field of computer hardware engineering.

Established with the vision of bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements, CHESA organizes workshops, seminars, technical competitions, and industry interactions to enhance the practical skills of students. Our association serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents, collaborate on projects, and stay updated with the latest advancements in hardware technology.

The PixelCraft 2025 inauguration marks the beginning of a new chapter for CHESA, with exciting events and initiatives planned throughout the year to empower students and prepare them for successful careers in the hardware engineering industry.
